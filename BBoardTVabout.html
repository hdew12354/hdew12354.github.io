BBoardTV

'"you can't make a thing in an hour" ~them'

so, this project was fully inspired by the rampant marching of time that creates the haunting concept of death that can only be kept at bay by listening to the deafening silence of boredom, and telling it 'no, i may be some infinitecimally small spec on the grand cosmic scale of time but i'm still gonna make a billboard.'

All jokes aside, this project was concieved, planned, developed, tested and published all within the span of just one hour. i decided to do this project as a 'time-trial' of sorts, in which i had to make an app and upload it within 1 hour. the main purpose of the app is to be a 'billboard' among the likes of the weird clock billboard things you sometimes see when there is an advertising billboard that nobody has bought ad space for.
