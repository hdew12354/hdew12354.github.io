I love to solve problems in interesting and effective ways, I love to Program and I Love to make Videos showing off my solutions to Those Problems.

One such video can be found on my Channel, GallowShot, in which I set out to solve the problem of me wanting to have an Amazon Alexa, but not having enough money to buy one, so I decided that, since there is the Alexa App on windows, that I could use that, as well as a Steam Link to run the Alexa App on a computer, and have the Steam Link pair up with a laptop and a USB microphone, and run a python script I wrote that actively searches the microphone's input for the word 'Alexa' or whatever I wanted it to look for, and emulate a certain Key-bind, that would activate the Alexa on the laptop, through the steam link, effectively building an Alexa!
